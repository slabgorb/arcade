## Title
Star Wars — ROM wireframe geometry (contact-sheet audit)

## Overview
The ROM|PORT model contact sheet (orchestrator `just bake-models star-wars`; star-wars PR 77) compares our shipped wireframes against the original 1983 MACRO-11 source. models.ts states plainly that its EDGES were reconstructed by heuristic — the disassembly held vertex tables but no draw lists. The original source (WSOBJ.MAC) ships BOTH halves, so the connectivity was recoverable all along. Audit findings: DARTH_TIE is largely fabricated (44 of its 104 edges appear nowhere in the ROM — models.ts admits authoring them — and 12 real ones are missing); the TIE family has small edge drift (TIE 1/3, TI1 1/0, TI2 1/0, TI3 3/0); and the ground objects (exhaust port, surface tower, surface bunker) cannot yet be compared at all, because their edges live in `.WGD` PLOT/DRAWTO routines the parser does not read. Vertices are NOT in question — they were ported correctly and match the ROM byte-for-byte; this epic is about edges (and, for the ground objects, vertices too). Design for the parser extension: docs/superpowers/specs/2026-07-12-wgd-ground-object-edges-design.md
