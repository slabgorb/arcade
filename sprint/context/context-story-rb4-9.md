## Problem
Cluster C8. Subsumes RD-001, RD-002, RD-004, RD-005, RD-006, RD-007, RD-008, RD-009, RD-010, OB-014. The ROM's most prominent foreground element — the player's own propeller, three sweeping blade arcs repatched into the display list EVERY VG FRAME at 62.5 Hz from seven pictures PROP0..PROP6 — IS NOT DRAWN. Neither is the enemy plane's prop (it is transcribed in topology.ts and never rendered — the biplanes fly with a bare nose). There is no depth-cued INTENSITY: the AVG has a 3-bit per-vector intensity and the ROM drives it from depth, and draws objects in TWO tiers (the airframe at V.BRIT, then the wing struts 0x60 dimmer — `;ADD LIGHTER LINES`, RBARON.MAC:5030-5040); we stroke the whole world in one flat green. Lives are tracked and never drawn. The windscreen bullet holes that accumulate as the ace shoots you are absent. The PLVALU readout (the live worth of the plane in your sights) is absent. Shells are DOTS in the ROM (VGDOT), not the line streaks we stroke. NOTE: OB-011 claimed the prop is NOT a 3-frame animation — the coverage review REFUTED that; it IS one (.PROPS is a six-entry JMPL table, 3 pictures x 2 VG buffers, and PLPROP patches one per frame). topology.ts's docstring is CORRECT; do not 'fix' it.
