## Problem
Replace the DEATH_STAR_SURFACE (Object_8) spike with a wide, procedurally-generated ground grid on the y=0 plane that recedes to a horizon and scrolls toward the cockpit — the Wave-2 surface skim. Object_8 is a narrow 3-fin structure (~960 wide, ~10,560 deep) seated 600 units from the eye, so it balloons off all screen edges and collapses to a point (verified by reprojection; see star-wars/docs/adr/0002-scene-geometry-surface-and-trench.md part A). Add a PURE core generator surfaceGrid(scroll) -> Model3D: longitudinal lines parallel to -Z wide enough to run off-screen at the horizon, lateral lines every GRID_Z scrolling toward the camera and recycling by scroll mod GRID_Z. The existing SURFACE_TOWER turrets keep their sim positions and sit on the grid. Retire Object_8 from the surface render (re-classify, don't delete). Add a surfaceScrollZ accumulator to GameState advanced by TURRET_SCROLL_SPEED and reset on phase entry. Depends on 11-4 for verification.
