## Problem
Replace the flat 512x384 TRENCH floor tile with a long walled channel that reads as a corridor you fly down. The current tile projects to a ~224px-wide, ~4px-tall sliver — no walls, no length (verified by reprojection; see star-wars/docs/adr/0002-scene-geometry-surface-and-trench.md part B). Add a PURE core generator trenchChannel(scroll) -> Model3D: floor rails at x=+/-TRENCH_HALF_W plus lateral floor ribs every RIB_Z, two vertical side walls from y=0 to y=TRENCH_WALL_H with a vertical rib at each RIB_Z, and top rails — running from ~z=0 to a far cutoff and scrolling toward the camera (recycling by scroll mod RIB_Z). The exhaust port keeps its own sim scroll and is seated in the floor at channel center. Add a trenchScrollZ accumulator to GameState advanced by TRENCH_SCROLL_SPEED and reset on phase entry. Camera unchanged (skims y=TRENCH_SKIM, centered). Depends on 11-4 for verification.
