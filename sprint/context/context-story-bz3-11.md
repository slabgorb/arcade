## Problem
Cluster C11. Subsumes C-004, C-005, S-021. The ROM cues the off-screen enemy two ways the clone half-implements: it FLASHES 'ENEMY IN RANGE' at ~3.9 Hz (C-004, gated by AND FRAME bit 1) where the clone draws it steadily, and it shows a directional 'ENEMY TO LEFT/RIGHT/REAR' callout (S-021, C-005) telling the player which way to turn toward the unseen enemy. The four direction strings are already transcribed in text.ts but alerts.ts only ever emits MOTION_BLOCKED and ENEMY_IN_RANGE — the directional half is dead. (The audio twin, WARNG, is bz3-10.)
