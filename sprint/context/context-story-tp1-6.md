## Problem
Cluster C3. Subsumes W-002, W-003, W-004, W-024, W-029, DA-012, DA-017. ONE OBJECT, NOT TWO CHANGES: the cap is not independently implementable. The ROM caps live invaders at 7 (NINVAD=7, WINVMX=6 for every wave 1-99) and regulates spawning by SLOT BACK-PRESSURE — nymphs simply stop advancing when the slots are booked. Without a nymph queue there is nowhere to hold the surplus, so adding the cap alone would silently drop spawns. Changes the difficulty of every wave. Also unblocks the fuseball turn-back (W-024) and the pulsar yo-yo (W-029), both keyed on NYMCOU.
