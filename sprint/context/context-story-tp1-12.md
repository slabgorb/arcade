## Problem
Cluster C7. Subsumes V-011, V-019, DA-005 (colour half), DB-010, DB-017. One indirection: replace `enemy -> fixed hex` with `enemy -> palette slot -> per-wave-group COLTAB bank`. Six banks, advancing every 16 waves. This is what makes the arcade's later waves look like different games, and it brings the INVISIBLE-WELL WAVES (65-80) — one of Tempest's best-known difficulty spikes, which we simply do not have — for free. The enemy-burst-white item is carved out to tp1-3.
