## Problem
Holding auto-fire while spinning only lands a shot every ~2-3 frames, so spin-and-hold skips enemies. AUTHENTIC ROM CONSTANTS (rev-3): there is NO fire cooldown — firing is gated only by (a) the fire button held and (b) a free shot slot; max 8 concurrent player shots (n_player_bullets=8); ONE new shot spawned per frame while held; shots travel +9 along-units/frame and free their slot at 0xf0 (~25 frames / ~0.42s lifetime from the rim). The ~1-per-3-frames steady state is authentic (8 slots / ~25-frame lifetime); the full-sweep feel comes from up to 8 shots in flight spread down the tube. FIX = remove any artificial cooldown, gate on an 8-shot concurrent cap, verify bullet speed so the pool recycles at the arcade rate. This answers the open cooldown-vs-cap question: it is the CAP, not a cooldown. Area: fire/cooldown logic in src/core/sim.ts; auto-fire input wiring in shell.
