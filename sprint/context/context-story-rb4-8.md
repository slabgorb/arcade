## Problem
Cluster C7. Subsumes MI-014, MI-015, MI-016, MI-018, MI-019. We GENERATE an even stagger with all four mountains at x=0; the ROM AUTHORS their placement in PFOBIZ (RBARON.MAC:1305-1306 — four initial depths and four initial X positions, all hex). The ROM runs TWO closing rates (P.OBDZ=0x180=384 while on the horizon, 0x20=32 once fallen); we have one. 'On horizon' is a LATCHED STATUS BIT with hysteresis (PFOBJ+6 D7), not a depth comparison. Mountains SCROLL LATERALLY with the player — the ROM subtracts PLYRDL from every free object's X each calc frame and wraps (RBARON.MAC:3298-3306); ours never do, which is a large part of why the world feels static. Mountains recycle to P.OBZI=0x7F00=32512, so they spend real time on the horizon; ours recycle to HORZ and never do.
