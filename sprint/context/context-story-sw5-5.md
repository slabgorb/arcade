## Acceptance Criteria
- SURFACE_TOWER vertices and edges come from ROM `STB` — the `.WGD STB` routine, "STUB OF TOWER WITHOUT BUNKER HAT ON TOP", which models.ts's own doc comment already names. Blocked on sw5-1 for the edges.

- SURFACE_BUNKER vertices and edges come from ROM `BNK` (`.WGD BNK`).
- TOWER_CAP is reconciled and documented. In the ROM, `TWR` (which IS `GND` — `.WGD TWR` is immediately followed by `.WGD2 GND`, one object with one draw routine) strokes the column AND the cap together. Our split into two models exists only because Canvas strokes one colour per drawWireframe call. Either merge them or state that reason in the doc comment — do not leave the split unexplained, since it reads as a fidelity error.
- Surface-phase collision still works with the corrected (taller) tower; any change in the collidable volume is called out.
- The contact sheet reports 0/0 drift for STB to Surface Tower and BNK to Surface Bunker.
