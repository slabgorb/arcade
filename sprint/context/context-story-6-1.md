## Problem
On level-clear the warp triggers instantly with no speed ramp, so if the last enemy killed leaves you on/near a spiked lane you die with no chance to react. Implement the authentic accelerating dive with retained control. AUTHENTIC ROM CONSTANTS (rev-3, see design ref): the level-clear dive runs under game_state_zoom — initial velocity 0x0200 = 2.0 along-units/frame, accelerating EVERY frame by min(curlevel*4,0x30)+0x20; total descent (along 0x10 -> 0xf0) is ~45 frames (~0.75s) at level 1 down to ~33 frames (~0.55s) by level 12+. The player keeps FULL rotation control for the entire descent (move_player runs every frame). A ~30-frame (~0.5s) AVOID SPIKES countdown precedes the dive when any spike exists AND displayed level <= 7 (no warning above 7). A spike kills only when the descending claw passes its tip on its OWN segment (spike height < player along). Map per-frame values to dt-driven per-second rates (60 Hz source); our depth is [0=far,1=near] so invert. Area: src/core/sim.ts level-transition + warp speed curve; render in shell.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Warp descent uses a slow-start, then fast-accelerating speed curve (not a constant/instant speed).
- After the last enemy dies, the player has a brief slow window to rotate off a spiked lane before the warp commits to descent.
- A player parked on a spiked lane when the last enemy dies can survive by moving off that lane during the slow phase.
- Warp timing is driven entirely by dt (deterministic, frame-rate independent) — covered by a seeded core unit test.
- Descent is an accelerating ramp (slow ~2.0 units/frame start, continuous accel, ~0.55-0.75s total); spinner stays live throughout; ~0.5s AVOID SPIKES warning on levels <=7 when spikes present; spike kills only on the player's own lane once passed.
