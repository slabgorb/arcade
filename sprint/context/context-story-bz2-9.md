## Problem
Playtest: the opening isn't hard, it's UNFAIR. Enemies spawn behind the player (outside the forward view cone), immediately open fire, and kill the player within ~2s of spawn — no time to turn or react. Not fun / not playable. This is spawn GEOMETRY + instant-fire, not a difficulty-ratchet volume problem. Fix: bias initial/respawn placement toward the forward view cone (or at least a reactable bearing) instead of the rear blind spot, and/or add a brief post-spawn grace before a newly spawned enemy can fire. Keep authentic ROM difficulty as the ceiling for later levels — this is about fair engagement geometry, not softening the ratchet.
