## Problem
Cluster C10. Subsumes OB-017, MI-008, OB-016, OB-018. 037007.XXX:1132-1230 defines a complete, self-contained ground-object set that topology.ts does not contain AT ALL: the point-sets PFPYRM (4 points), PFHOME (7), PFTANK (10), PFPBOX (16); the decode-lists DEPFPY/DEPFHS/DEPFTK/DEPFPB; the PFODEC pointer table; the PFLOB point-set table; the .PFLOB length table; and the PFOFFS table of 12 screen offsets. A ground wave deploys exactly two mountain target-groups (GRNDCT=2, GTIMER). The blimp's 8-vertex collision box BLCOLL (RBARON.MAC:6270-6277) is the ONLY member of the PLNDB master table missing from topology.ts. REMEMBER: this region of 037007.XXX is `.RADIX 10` — transcribe it as DECIMAL, exactly as the existing (correct) topology.ts transcription does. Do NOT apply rb4-1's hex correction here; that would break it.
