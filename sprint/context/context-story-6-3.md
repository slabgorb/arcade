Safe respawn after death (no chain-death on death lanes)

## Problem
On death the next ship respawns instantly at the death spot, so you chain-die on a blocked/crowded lane. DESIGN DECISION RESOLVED from the ROM (rev-3): the arcade does NOT use invulnerability — it RESETS THE BOARD. On death player_status goes negative; the death animation waits for all bullets to clear, then pushes surviving enemies back down the tube and zooms the claw down (along 0x10 -> 0xf0, +0x0f/frame ~= 15 frames / ~0.25s) — the enemies-vanish/claw-freezes/warp-to-blank the playtest observed. Then a life is deducted (40-frame / ~0.67s GAME OVER pause if it was the last); with lives remaining the board is FULLY RESET (remove_all_enemies_from_tube + setup_level + reset_pending_enemy_timers + clear_shots) and the player respawns at a FIXED lane (arcade segment 14), near rim, on the SAME level. NO invulnerability frames — the cleared board + spawn delay IS the grace, which is why the arcade never chain-deaths. Implement that model. Area: death/lives -> respawn transition in src/core/sim.ts.
