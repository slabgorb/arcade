# Epic 6 Context

## Title
Wave 6 — Playtest feel & balance

## Overview
Gameplay feel and fairness fixes surfaced during hands-on playtest (post Waves 0-5). Warp instant-death, auto-fire cadence, and respawn chain-death.

## Metadata
- **Epic ID:** 6
- **Repo:** tempest

## Architecture & Reuse
**No new systems.** Every story here extends tempest's existing deterministic
core. The hard boundary still holds: changes land in `tempest/src/core/`
(pure, test-first) and are *rendered/heard* by `tempest/src/shell/`. Fairness and
feel are **simulation** properties — fix them in `core/sim.ts` and `core/rules.ts`
with failing tests first, not by tweaking the render loop.

Two flavours of story in this epic:
- **Fairness fixes** (6-3, 6-5) — behavioural changes to the sim. High value, P1.
- **Authenticity passes** (6-6 … 6-9) — replace approximations with data faithful
  to the original 1981 cabinet. P2 polish.

### Behavioural ground truth (external)
- **Original Atari Tempest (1981)** via **MAME** — the reference for "authentic"
  geometry (6-7), shapes (6-8), and motion/behaviour constants (6-9). When the
  approximation and the original disagree, the original wins; log the change.
- Note: unlike epic 8, there is **no bundled Tempest disassembly** in this repo —
  authenticity work draws on observed original behaviour, not a local quarry.

## Cross-Story Constraints & Guardrails
1. **Determinism preserved.** New mechanics (enemy bolts, respawn timing) read
   `dt` and the seeded RNG only. No wall-clock, no `Math.random()`.
2. **Fairness before fidelity.** 6-3 and 6-5 are P1 (the game is currently
   *unfair* — chain-death on respawn; enemies that don't shoot back). The P2
   authenticity passes do not block them.
3. **Behaviour in core, looks in shell.** 6-8 is render-only; 6-5/6-9 are core.
   Don't smuggle game logic into `render.ts`.
4. **Audio assets uncommitted.** Baked SFX live under `sfx/` (gitignored) and are
   served separately — same rule as the rest of the arcade.
